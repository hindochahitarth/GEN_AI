MentorAI

Your Personal AI Teacher & Career Guide

The Problem

Lack of Personal Guidance

74% of students can't afford coaching. 1:30 faculty-student ratio leaves students without personalized mentorship, leading to confusion and suboptimal learning paths.

Outdated Syllabus

Curricula may not align with current industry trends, leaving students unprepared for real-world challenges and emerging technologies.

Placement Confusion

Navigating career options and job placements can be overwhelming without expert guidance, resulting in poor career choices and missed opportunities.

The Solution

MentorAI is an AI-powered personal teacher and career coach designed specifically for college students. It provides 24/7 personalized guidance, adaptive learning plans, and career support to help you excel academically and professionally.
