MentorAI

Your Personal AI Teacher & Career Guide

The Problem

Lack of Personal Guidance

College students often struggle without individualized mentorship, leading to confusion and suboptimal learning paths.

Outdated Syllabus

Curricula may not align with current industry trends, leaving students unprepared for real-world challenges.

Placement Confusion

Navigating career options and job placements can be overwhelming without expert guidance.

The Solution

MentorAI is an AI-powered personal teacher and career coach designed specifically for college students. It provides 24/7 personalized guidance, adaptive learning plans, and career support to help you excel academically and professionally.

Our Vision

To provide every college student in India with a personal AI mentor, democratizing access to quality education and career guidance through cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology.
